About Me

My name is Alex and I'm facinated with computers, small and large. From corporate cloud servers to mini integrated systems, if you can write and deploy code on it then I'm already brainstorming about what cool stuff can be built. For me, theres no feeling that can compare to the satisfaction of designing an amazing software solution that tackles a problem, then optimizing and perfecting the code until its flawless.

As a Software Engineer, I've taken an interest to mobile platforms, both Android and iOS. I believe apps for smartphones phones have a bright future in the computing world and that amazing potential has drawn me to this field. Through personal preference I have taken a liking to Android and hope to one day master the platform.

Procore

Procore is an all-in-one construction management software platform with a web and mobile app. While working at Procore I contributed countless bug fixes, many refactors, and several large new features to the Android app.

Noteworth accomplishments include: Architected and implemented background data syncing engine to existing codebase. Refactored legacy spaghetti code into performant, testable and organized MVVM pattern. Introduced and advocated reactive paradigms to the project, mainly with Reactive Extensions (Rx) libraries.

  • Store: Google Play, iTunes
  • Date: January 2018 - June 2020
  • Client: Procore

Jane

Jane is an online womens fashion and apparel store similar to Amazon.com. They also feature daily deals and flash sales with unbeatable prices.

Jane had an iOS app and they needed an Android app to match it, so I worked with their designer and one other developer to create the first version of the app from the ground up. The Android app uses the PayPal SDK for secure online payments, neatly connects to their existing web service, and even supports special layouts for tablets.

  • Store: Google Play
  • Date: Winter 2014 - Spring 2015
  • Client: Jane

Sudo

Sudo is a semi anonymous telephony, SMS and email app for protecting your privacy. You can create 'pseudo' identities to hide your main phone number and email.

I contributed to this app by adding several new features to their existing codebase for the release of a new version. Additions include email attachment functionality, updated identity card views, several new animations, and changes to the phone calling screen.

  • Store: iTunes
  • Date: Summer - Fall 2016
  • Client: Anonyome Labs
